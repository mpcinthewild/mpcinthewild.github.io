MPC in the Wild

Multi-Party Computation Research Project

About

MPC in the Wild is a research project exploring the practical applications and implementations of Multi-Party Computation (MPC) in real-world scenarios.

Multi-Party Computation is a cryptographic protocol that enables multiple parties to jointly compute a function over their inputs while keeping those inputs private. This research investigates how MPC technologies are being deployed and utilized in production environments.

Research Focus

  • Practical deployment of MPC protocols
  • Performance analysis in real-world settings
  • Security considerations and challenges
  • Use cases and applications
